# Profit Optimization & Scenario Analysis (Excel)

## Business Problem
To evaluate profitability of apparel orders and analyse sensitivity of profit to pricing and production cost changes.

## Data Overview
Order-level data including quantity, making cost, making charge, total minutes, revenue, margin, and profit.

## Analysis Performed
- KPI tracking (Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Minutes)
- Descriptive statistics (mean, median, standard deviation, skewness)
- Price sensitivity analysis using what-if tables
- Conditional formatting to highlight margin risk

## Key Insight
Profitability shows higher sensitivity to pricing (making charge) than to production cost, indicating strong pricing leverage.

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
